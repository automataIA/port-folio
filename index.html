About Me

Hi, I'm Marco Rossi, an engineer specialized in AI and Machine Learning with a passion for solving complex problems through data analysis and intelligent algorithms.

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and a Master's in Data Science from Stanford University. Over the past 5 years, I've worked with various Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups to implement AI solutions that have transformed their decision-making processes.

La mia esperienza spazia dall'elaborazione del linguaggio naturale (NLP) alla computer vision, dal deep learning al reinforcement learning. Sono particolarmente interessato alle applicazioni dell'AI nell'healthcare, nella finanza e nella sostenibilità ambientale.
